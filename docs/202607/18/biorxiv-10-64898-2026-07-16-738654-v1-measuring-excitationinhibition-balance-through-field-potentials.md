## 摘要
几种基于脑电图的指标已被提出用于指示兴奋/抑制平衡（E/I）。我们使用大鼠内侧前额叶和眶额皮层以及小鼠视觉皮层和海马区的单单位与局部场电位记录，评估了四种候选指标与实验测量的E/I的关系。虽然1/f斜率与伽马振荡表现出区域和情境依赖性，但宽带（6-80 Hz）和高伽马（80-150 Hz）功率与E/I持续相关，支持将其作为替代指标的使用。

## Abstract
Several electroencephalography-based metrics have been proposed to index excitation/inhibition balance (E/I). We used single-unit and local field potential recordings from rat medial prefrontal and orbitofrontal cortex and mouse visual cortex and hippocampus to evaluate four candidate metrics against empirically-measured E/I. While 1/f slope and gamma oscillations showed region and context dependencies, broadband (6-80 Hz) and high gamma (80-150 Hz) power consistently correlated with E/I, supporting their use as proxy measures.
