chunked into one unit if the product of normalised probability and reliability exceeds a certain chunk constant, i.e., when both the transi4onal probability and the reliability of the

Second, we varied the threshold that the product of probability and reliability has to reach

constant had a high tendency to chunk and quickly segmented the tone sequence according

tendency to chunk.
